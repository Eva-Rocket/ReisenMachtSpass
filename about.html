About Me

Hallo, ich bin Sarah!

I enjoy traveling (obv), surfing, jogging, reading, Disneyland trips, and playing with my Canon Rebel t7 camera.

Traveling is the best way to learn about culture, cuisine, religion, spirtuality, and sport. I have been an avid traveler since junior college and I make it a point to make at least one trip every year.

Since 2005 I have visited England (4 times), Canada (4 times), France (twice), Germany (twice), Austria, Czech Republic, several Greek islands, Turkey, and Russia.

Because of my close proximity to Mexico I have visited Baja California more times than I can count. I also lived in Alaska for 5 years!
